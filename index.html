About

Stereotypical Anime Protagonist Backstory.

  • 14 Sep 2020

    Humble Beginnings

    It started as a joke to make a Sakura Miko clicker app like Tuturu by DerVizegrafAch. However due to my lack of knowledge with the Xamarin language the project had many issues and failed like most kickstarters.

  • 15 October 2020

    Published

    After a month of hardwork learning Java and app development. I published my 1st app Kiara Noises on the Google Play Store.

  • 13 October 2020

    A Massive Success

    As Kiara Noises was a success, we made a Vtuber Noises platform based on it. This allowed us to build Gura Noises quickly and effectively which made it such a massive success with 1k+ downloads.

  • 23 December 2020

    An Upgrade

    As we move on, VTubers make more noises, we have to make our apps updated.That's why we developed Vtuber Noises 2.0 and from that created our 3rd app Polka Noises

Kiara Noises

My 1st Published App

Phoenix noises made by Tenchou during her streams.Currently features sounds such as "Eat me!", "I offer my body to you", "Muah" and many more. Launched as a prototype app which lead to the creation of Vtuber Noises platform and subsequent apps.

  • Status: Active
  • Date: 15 October 2020
  • Category: App Development

Gura Noises

A Massive Success

Shark noises made by Gawr Gura during her streams.Currently features sounds such as "A","NOOOOO!" and most importantly "Ara Ara". Built using the Vtuber Noises platform derived from our Kiara Noises project. It was able to get 1K+ Downloads.

  • Status: Active
  • Date: 31 October 2020
  • Category: App Development

Polka Noises

An Upgrade

Fennec noises made by the Clown during her streams.Currently features sounds such as "Selamat Pagi", "Pol!", "Polka Oruka?" and many more. With more noises. a newer platform is required to support all features, it was built using the Vtuber Noises 2.0 Platform with a better randomizer and dark mode friendly UI.

  • Status: Active
  • Date: 23 December 2020
  • Category: App Development
