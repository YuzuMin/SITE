About

Stereotypical Anime Protagonist Backstory.

  • 14 Sep 2020

    Humble Beginnings

    It started as a joke to make a Sakura Miko clicker app like Tuturu by DerVizegrafAch. However due to my lack of knowledge with the Xamarin language the project had many issues and failed like most kickstarters.

  • 15 October 2020

    Published

    After a month of hardwork learning Java and app development. I published my 1st app Kiara Noises on the Google Play Store.

  • 13 October 2020

    A Massive Success

    As Kiara Noises was a success, we made a hololive Noises platform based on it. This allowed us to build Gura Noises quickly and effectively which made it such a massive success with 1k+ downloads.

  • 23 December 2020

    An Upgrade

    As we move on, VTubers make more noises, we have to make our apps updated.That's why we developed hololive Noises 2.0 and from that created our 3rd app Polka Noises
